About Me
I am Thosphere. I combine a professional approach to visual craft with a studio practice that embraces experimentation. My work ranges from focused character studies to small interactive prototypes and layout experiments.
Primary tools: Clip Studio, Procreate, Roblox Studio
Focus: Character design, color studies, compact UX
I began exploring digital art through simple sketches and small projects. Over time I focused on making work that reads well at small sizes, that tells a mood using color and silhouette. I care about clarity: every shape should communicate something. I also enjoy the craft of building compact web pages and simple interactions that present work neatly.

Practice & Process

I start with thumbnails and rough shapes, iterate with values, then add color and details. I keep experiments short and intentional so learning is quick and visible. When a study clicks, I refine and archive the process for later reflection.

Projects & Goals

  • Finish a small character pack that explores consistent lighting approaches.
  • Polish a playable prototype with readable silhouettes and responsive feedback.
  • Keep an ongoing notebook of color palettes and small compositional studies.
