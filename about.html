About Me
I’m Thosphere. I draw, design, and mess around with digital art until it starts looking cool. I like mixing simple ideas with a little twist, its nothing too deep.. trust me, just fun stuff that feels like me. Most of the time I’m sketching characters, testing colors, or building tiny things inside Roblox Studio for no real reason other than “what if.. ?”
Primary tools: Clip Studio, Procreate, Roblox Studio
Focus: Character design, color studies, compact UX
I started out doodling random characters and trying colors that absolutely didn’t match. Eventually I figured out what works (kinda). Now I focus on making things feel clean, readable, and a little strange if you look long enough. I like experimenting—small projects, tiny prototypes, quick studies. They help me learn faster than trying to make some huge masterpiece. I also enjoy making simple websites and small interactions because it’s fun seeing something you made actually move and work on screen.

Practice & Process

I usually start with messy shapes, then try to make them look like real things. After that I test colors, fix mistakes, break things on accident, fix them again, and eventually finish something I like. If something turns out good, I save it. If it turns out terrible, I still save it (so I can laugh at it later).

Projects & Goals

  • Build a small character pack with consistent lighting… or at least close enough.
  • Actually make a tiny playable prototype that doesn't completely bugs the whole thing..
  • Try more experiments, or praciticing something without overthinking everything (i guess).
